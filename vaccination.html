Animal Vaccination Details

Animals Age Vaccination Details
Cattle 6-12 months
  • Clostridial Diseases: Initial dose at 2-4 months, booster 4-6 weeks later. Annual boosters.
  • Respiratory Diseases: IBR, BVD, PI3, BRSV: Initial dose at 2-4 months, booster 4-6 weeks later. Annual boosters.
  • Leptospirosis: Annual vaccination, especially in areas with a history of leptospirosis.
  • Brucellosis: Often done at a specific age, depending on local regulations.
  • Parasite Control: Regular deworming based on herd health and environmental factors.
Dog 3-6 months
  • Bordetella (Kennel Cough): Frequency depends on risk, often annually.
  • Leptospirosis: Initial series, then annual boosters.
  • Lyme Disease: Dependent on geographic risk, may be annual.
Sheep 2-4 months
  • Clostridial Diseases: Initial dose at 6-8 weeks, booster at 10-12 weeks. Annual boosters.
  • Footrot: Vaccination based on regional risk, often annually.
  • Parasite Control: Regular deworming as per regional guidelines.
Goat 3-6 months
  • Clostridial Diseases: Initial dose at 6-8 weeks, booster at 10-12 weeks. Annual boosters.
  • CAE (Caprine Arthritis Encephalitis): Region-specific vaccination protocols.
  • Parasite Control: Regular deworming as per regional guidelines.
Chicken 1-2 months
  • Marek's Disease: Day-old chicks in the hatchery.
  • Newcastle Disease: Initial dose at 2-3 weeks, booster at 6 weeks. Annual boosters.
  • Infectious Bronchitis: Initial dose at 2-3 weeks, booster at 6 weeks. Annual boosters.
Horse 6-12 months
  • Tetanus: Initial dose at 6 months, booster 4-6 weeks later. Annual boosters.
  • Equine Influenza: Initial dose at 6 months, booster 4-6 weeks later. Annual boosters.
  • West Nile Virus: Initial dose at 6 months, booster 4-6 weeks later. Annual boosters.
Pig 2-4 months
  • Mycoplasma Hyopneumoniae: Initial dose at 1-2 weeks, booster at 3-4 weeks. Annual boosters.
  • Porcine Circovirus: Initial dose at 2-3 weeks, booster at 4-5 weeks. Annual boosters.
  • Swine Influenza: Initial dose at 3-4 weeks, booster at 5-6 weeks. Annual boosters.
Rabbit 2-4 months
  • Myxomatosis: Initial dose at 6 weeks, booster at 12 weeks. Annual boosters.
  • Rabbit Haemorrhagic Disease: Initial dose at 10 weeks, booster at 14 weeks. Annual boosters.
Cat 3-6 months
  • Feline Herpesvirus: Initial dose at 8-10 weeks, booster at 12-14 weeks. Annual boosters.
  • Feline Calicivirus: Initial dose at 8-10 weeks, booster at 12-14 weeks. Annual boosters.
  • Feline Panleukopenia: Initial dose at 8-10 weeks, booster at 12-14 weeks. Annual boosters.
Duck 1-2 months
  • Duck Viral Enteritis: Initial dose at 2-3 weeks, booster at 6 weeks. Annual boosters.
  • Duck Hepatitis: Initial dose at 2-3 weeks, booster at 6 weeks. Annual boosters.
